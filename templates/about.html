About Judge Port

The Russia-Ukraine war began on February 24, 2022, when Russia launched a full-scale invasion of Ukraine. Since then, the conflict has caused immense human suffering, economic disruption, and geopolitical tension. Millions of people were displaced from their homes, cities were destroyed, families were separated, and thousands lost their lives.

In this complex and emotionally charged environment, Judge Port serves as an AI-powered platform designed to analyze public comments related to the war. It classifies these comments into Positive, Neutral, or Negative using natural language processing and sentiment analysis.

This tool not only supports fact-based reporting and policy development but also plays a vital role in detecting harmful narratives, preventing the spread of misinformation, and encouraging respectful discourse.

Our goal is to help uncover public sentiment trends in real time during major world events.
